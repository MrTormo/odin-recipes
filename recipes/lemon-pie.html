Lemon Pie

Description

Ingredients

  • Sablee Dough
    • Butter 150g
    • Flour 250g
    • Egg yolks 3
    • Powdered sugar 110g
    • Vanilla flavoring to taste
  • Swiss Meringue
    • Egg whites 4
    • Sugar 150g
    • Lemon juice a few drops
  • Lemon filling
    • Milk 500ml
    • Lemon juice 200ml
    • Butter 50g
    • Cornstarch 60g
    • Egg yolks 4
    • Sugar 200g
    • Lemon cest 1 lemon's worth

Steps

  1. For the dough:
    1. Mix the cold butter (previously cut in small cubes) with the sugar and flour, without heating it up too much, until you've got a grainy texture
    2. Add the yolks, vanilla and lemon cest. Mix until it's homogeneous.
    3. Cover the dough with film, let it rest in the fridge at least 1 hour.
    4. Take the cold dough and make a ball, sprinkle the table with flour and stretch the dough in a circle 4mm thick
    5. Roll the stretched dough on top of the mold, making it take its shape, taking special care on the edges of the pan. Let it rest at least 30 minutes in the fridge before baking.
  2. For the lemon filling:
    1. Place the milk with half the sugar on a pot and bring to the heat.
    2. Place the previously sieved corn starch in a bowl along with the rest of the sugar, the egg yolks, the lemon juice and zest , and half of the hot milk, mix and leave the other half on the heat. When the rest of the milk boils, pour the yolk mixture over it, cook while continuously mixing and once it starts to boil again cook for 1 more minute until thickened.
    3. Remove from the heat and add the butter, mix and let it cool.
  3. For the swiss meringue:
    1. Heat the egg whites with the sugar over a bain-marie to 45°C, stirring constantly, then remove from heat and beat until firm peaks form. Add a squeeze of lemon juice that will only contribute to making the meringue whiter and shinier.
  4. Putting it all together:
    1. Prick the cold tart with a fork so that the dough does not swell, then cover with aluminum foil, copying the shape of the dough and fill the mold with ceramic balls or beans. (the extra weight will prevent the dough from deforming in the bake)
    2. Bake in a preheated oven at 170ºC for 15 minutes, until it takes on a slightly toasted color.
    3. Remove and let cool.
    4. Once the lemon filling is cooler, pour it over the cake.
    5. Cover the lemon filling with the meringue.
  5. Presentation:
    1. Sprinkle the meringue with sugar and burn with a blowtorch.
    2. Cut into portions.
  6. Enjoy!
