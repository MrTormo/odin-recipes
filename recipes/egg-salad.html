Egg Salad

Description

A classic summer salad, this healthy alternative to the usual egg salad will make you wish summer lasts forever.

Ingredients

  • Hard-boiled eggs, divided 12
  • Mayonnaise 1/4 cup
  • Chives, fresh, chopped 1/4 cup
  • Dijon Mustard 1 teaspoon
  • Celery Salt 1/2 teaspoon
  • Paprika, ground 1/2 teaspoon
  • Salt 1/2 teaspoon
  • Black pepper, freshly ground 1/2 teaspoon

Steps

  1. Peel and chop 8 eggs; place in a medium bowl.
  2. Peel remaining 4 eggs. Cut each in half to remove yolks, reserving them for another use. Chop whites and add to the bowl.
  3. Mix in mayonnaise, chives, Dijon, celery salt, paprika, kosher salt, and pepper until salad is well combined.
  4. Enjoy!
